In these C programs, the user is prompted to enter a height, and a triangle of the input height is printed using hashes('#').


In right_aligned_right_triangle.c, a right angled triangle aligned to the right side is printed.

Example -->        
             #         
            ##
           ###
          ####
         #####
        ######
       #######
      ########
                   
This is a right aligned right triangle of height 8.


In double_triangle.c, two triangles, one right aligned and one left aligned, are printed together with a gap of 2 spaces.

Example -->

        #  #
       ##  ##
      ###  ###
     ####  ####

These are two triangles of height '4'.
