Teal Cafe Menu

Single Espresso Shot  -->   Just place the cup on the espresso machine and voila!

Double Espresso Shot  --> Place the single espresso shot on the espresso machine again to get a double shot

Milk  -->  Get the milk from the fridge

Frothed Milk  -->  Use the steam wand on the espresso machine to froth the milk

Cappuccino  -->  Pour the frothed milk into the single espresso shot

Latte Art  -->  We can make a flower or a heart design on the cappuccino according to customers likings. Just take the cappuccino to the flower or heart station

Dark Coffee  -->  Combine the double shot of espresso with plain milk to get Dark Coffee

Mocha  -->  Add chocolate syrup to dark coffee to make Cafe Mocha
